Instructions:

Welcome to Pong! The goal of this game is to score the most points against your opponenent. When the ball hits the opponents wall, the opposing side will get a point. The left player will use the WS keys and the right player will use the up and down keys. After three points for one player, the speed of the ball will increase. After 5 points, the leading side will get another ball. The player to get to 7 points wins!
